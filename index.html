About

I'm a visual artist based in Bangalore – India, working across painting, digital media, and installation. My practice explores quiet tension — the way light moves through a room, how colour shifts with time of day, the brief pause before a gesture completes.

I've shown with galleries in Kochi, Chennai, and a few other locations across India. I also teach drawing and at painting in my Bangalore studio and run a monthly open-studio night.
